## 2025-10-27 - Keyboard Navigation in Immersive UIs
**Learning:** Heavy visual effects (custom cursors, parallax) often mask the lack of basic keyboard affordances like focus states and skip links.
**Action:** When seeing a "creative" cursor, immediately verify if the underlying interactive elements remain accessible to keyboard users.
