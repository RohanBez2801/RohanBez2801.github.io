## 2025-10-26 - Navigation on Long Single-Page Sites
**Learning:** Long single-page portfolios often neglect return-to-top navigation, forcing users to scroll excessively to access navigation menus.
**Action:** Always include a "Back to Top" button on pages that exceed 2 screen heights.
