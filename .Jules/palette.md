## 2025-10-26 - Semantic Structure vs. Visual Design
**Learning:** Even highly visual portfolios with custom cursors often miss basic accessibility features like "Skip to content" links, which are critical for keyboard navigation.
**Action:** Always verify keyboard navigation paths (Tab key) and ensure a visible skip link is the first focusable element, especially when custom cursors or heavy animations are present.
